Short Bio & Curriculum Vitae

I am working as a Research Engineer in Artificial Intelligence Center at Samsung Research. Concurrently, I am Ph.D. Student in the Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST). I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jaegul Choo. I received M.E. under Prof. Jaegul Choo in the Department of Big Data Convergence at Korea University (KU). I earned B.S. under Prof. Jeehyong Lee in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SungKyunKwan University (SKKU).

Most interested in deep-learning, especially for natural language processing. In detail, my research mainly focuses on building algorithms to teach machines how to read and comprehend written texts.

Keywords: AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, MRC, Text Summarization
